elphbolt

Electron-phonon coupling Transport

General information

Code homepage: https://github.com/nakib/elphbolt

Full documentation: https://github.com/nakib/elphbolt

Detailed information

Short description: elphbolt (short for electron-phonon Boltzmann transport) is a modern Fortran (2018 standard) suite of transport codes. It provides a solver for both the coupled and decoupled electron and phonon Boltzmann transport equations (BTEs)
